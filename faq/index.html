What is a smart home/office/building?

 

A smart home is a home outfitted with devices that can be controlled by devices like smartphones or tablets. These connected devices can be appliances, lights, security systems, cameras, audio and video systems, televisions and even sprinklers.

Generally speaking, if your home has devices that connect to each other and a network, it’s a smart home. The complexity of smart systems may differ somewhat from home to home, but the basic foundation is the same.

Smart home basics: There are hundreds of devices on the markets that can make up a smart home system. Basic elements can now be automated or controlled from a smartphone anywhere in the world.

If you have a reliable internet connection and a few devices connected to it, you’re a part of the smart home revolution.

 

What are the benefits of automating a home or office?

 

The benefits of automation typically fall into a few categories, including savings, safety, convenience, and control. Additionally, some consumers purchase automation for comfort and peace of mind.

Savings: Smart products save energy.

How? We can always know where is the energy wastage happening and we can always prevent the same.

Safety: Automation definitely makes your place safer and more secure. Security cameras offer benefits through either remote monitoring of package deliveries or real-time video of your place inhabitants or unwanted visitors.

Convenience: Because Automation technologies perform routine tasks automatically, end users experience great convenience.

Control: Consumers also choose smart devices to better control functions within your place. With automation technology, you can know what’s happening inside your place at all times.

Comfort: Automation can also help create a comfortable atmosphere- they provide intelligent and adaptive lighting, sound, etc which can all help create an inviting environment.

Peace of mind: Many consumers invest in automation technology for peace of mind. Parents can check on their little ones from smart cameras and other technologies. You can check whether you have closed your main gate, or have switched off all lighting appliances before you leave.
