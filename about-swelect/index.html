About Swelect

Who we are

Swelect is a one-stop discovery platform for those who wish to build a smart, sustainable and energy efficient space which is always connected on the go.

Swelect stands for selection of smart devices that can monitor and control a space using electrical and electronic connectivity.

{ ‘Sw’– switch to a smart lifestyle; ‘elect’– by selecting electronics from our platform.}

What we do

We at Swelect would provide choice to our visitors in our search library for selecting tried, tested and proven products primarily from renowned brands “Made in India” and a combination of some other world leading brands for building a wholesome smart ecosystem in and around a space.

Keeping in mind the impact on the environment due to rising development, we also aim in reducing the carbon footprint of our client by providing smart building materials and products for energy conservation.

Our Team

We have a strong network of electricians, system integrators and technical experts from the IoT domain.

 

  • Our Mission – “To continuously identify, test and offer the best smart products and materials by updating them on our discovery platform for the betterment of the rising development scenario in the country and contribute in building a futuristic infrastructure”.

 

  • Our Vision –  “To build Smart, Sustainable & Secured Spaces for all”.

 

Smart – Integration of smart devices which will add intelligence to your space, improvising overall work efficiency in operations in commercial spaces from the utility point of view and increasing levels of convenience and luxury in the residential spaces from a comfort and security point of view. (home/ office/ building).

Sustainable – This intelligence helps improve the usage of the appliances or device connected and increases its overall life and reduces physical wear and tear. We also promote smart building materials which help conserve energy and help build a green space.

Secured – Security is a great concern when it comes to building a space. Keeping in mind the security and safety of a 5-year-old kid, a V.I.P (businessman, celebrity, politician or a person who requires added protection), a person with any physical disability or a 70-year-old individual who might visit the space at any point of time. We also provide security and monitoring solutions for people to keep their pets safe in their absence. Yes, we love the four-legged species equally!!

Spaces – Our offerings are for all types of spaces;

  1. Residential Solutions [Affordable living(single room), luxury living (whole home and farmhouses etc.), gated housing communities]
  2. Commercial Solutions [Green Buildings, Offices, Real Estate, Warehouses, Industries]
  3. Hospitality Solutions [Hotels and restaurants, hospitals and clinics, convention centre and auditoriums]

Our Vision is thus an outcome of a number of small inter-woven goals and mission of our companies objectives.

With SWELECT Home Automation, the possibilities for your space are limited only by your imagination!

A big welcome from our family to your start of a smart journey in building your dream project with us.
