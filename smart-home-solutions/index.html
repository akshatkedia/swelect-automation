What makes a Home SMART?

Control your property with your fingertips

Is it the people who occupy the house or the designers who build it or the technology that comes along in different gadgets or the building material used during construction; well it is a combination of all that helps build a SMART HOME ecosystem !!!

How?

The occupants or the owner of the space should accept to live in a space that is smart and that can reduce human dependency to a level where it can take care of itself in some of the most routine activities.

Only after the willingness of the owner the architect can design the space considering the factors required for designing and building a smart home.

There are a lot of types of smart home concepts to choose one out of these is the most crucial part. This depends on various factors; some of the basic to be considered is to what extent you care about the environment or your personal health, what are your priorities, do you want to implement automation as a utility or luxury and ultimately how much do you wish to spend on building a smart home. While we keep on using the term “smart home” the technology or the overall ecosystem is not restricted to just homes. Automation replaces a lot of redundant manual work in industries, hotels, restaurants, offices, auditoriums or cinema theatres so the application is for all types of spaces. Although the solutions vary in each of these sectors depending on what the requirement is.

We at Swelect provide personalized genuine solutions based on these factors and more.
